# Privacy Policy

Evolve Loop does not collect, transmit, or store any user data. All operations run locally on your machine using your existing AI CLI session.

## What Evolve Loop accesses

- **Your local codebase** — to scan, build, and audit improvements
- **Your local git history** — to create worktrees and commit changes
- **Local `.evolve/` directory** — to store cycle state, instincts, and logs (per-project, never shared)

## What Evolve Loop does NOT do

- No telemetry or analytics
- No network requests (except git push to your own remote and optional web research via your AI CLI)
- No data sent to third parties
- No user tracking or identification
- No cookies or local storage outside the project directory

## Third-party services

Evolve Loop runs within your AI CLI (Claude Code or Gemini CLI). Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies:

- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
