**Accepted-by-design (Phase 0 review, not fixed — recorded so they are explicit choices):**
`NamesReferencedInTests` collects all idents/selectors (broad "named" signal) — intentional: it is the AST half of a *two-signal* check; a method sharing a name with a common local (e.g. `Error`) may be over-credited, acceptable for warning-only tooling and revisited if Phase 5 needs `go/types`. · `Enumerate` measures against the ambient `build.Default` GOOS/GOARCH — intentional (matches what `go test` compiles on that host). · `CoverEntry.Func` is parsed but not used in the join — kept as the parser's correctness anchor (asserted by `cover_test.go`) and for future import-path qualification.
